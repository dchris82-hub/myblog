## 📘 Technical Architecture & Disclaimers

To operate purely client-side without a backend database, this tool relies on a series of robust engineering approximations rather than the full IAPWS-IF97 regional polynomials.

* **Saturation Curve:** Uses the **Wagner Equation** with a stable Bisection method solver.
* **Liquid Phase:** Modeled using continuous **AIChE DIPPR 105** curves (for density) and integrated $C_p$ polynomials.
* **Vapor Phase:** Incorporates the **Redlich-Kwong Equation of State (RK-EOS)** exclusively for compressibility ($Z$-factor) correction in vapor density. *(A warning is displayed if the numerical solver reverts to an ideal-gas fallback.)*
* **Enthalpy & Entropy:** Referenced to liquid water at 273.16 K. Superheated values are approximated via isobaric integration of ideal gas specific heat from the saturated state, without full EOS departure functions.
